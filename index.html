Research Interests

My research focuses on multivariate electrophysiological responses and biologically inspired network modeling techniques to uncover insights into brain function in health and disease. In addition to exploring the functional neuroanatomy of the visual system, my interests extend to applied biomedical research for social good and fostering inclusive sustainable ecosystems.
